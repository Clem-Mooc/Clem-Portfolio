About Clem

Hi, my name is Clem. I'm currently residing in Seattle, Washington. I went to school for Marketing Analytics and Information Systems (and minoring in Informatics) at the University of Washington. Even with a background in business and data, I've always had an interest in design.

When it comes to design, I like to create things that are practical and intuitive, but packed with enough content. I've been able to apply this methodology in many forms, including a prototype app, an infographic, and commercial landing pages.

I hope to delve into more design-related projects, where I can leverage my business and data background with design principles to meet business objectives.

When it comes to play, I enjoy photography, backpacking, brewing a hot drink, and typical gorp stuff.
